About Us

Miss. Gangamma is the founder of Shravani Ladies Tailor.
Hi I am a skilled Ladies tailor with
**20 years of experience in the tailoring field**.
During this period, I have worked in many reputed boutiques
and
gained special experience in different designs and modern fashion styles.
Later, I started my own tailoring shop and earned the trust of customers by providing
**perfect fitting and beautiful stitching**
according to each woman’s body measurements.
Giving importance to customers’ preferences, comfort, and design,
I carefully create every outfit with elegance and attractiveness.
The main specialties of my work:
* Perfect fitting according to measurements
* Neat and precise stitching
* Experience in modern fashion designs
* Customer-friendly service
* Discipline and trust in delivering orders on the promised time
 With the belief that
**“Customer satisfaction is my success,”**
I provide my service with love, dedication, and responsibility.
